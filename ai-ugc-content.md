# AI UGC Content – Performance-Driven Video

## Goal
Create user-generated style content using AI tools, designed for engagement, authenticity, and conversion on social media.

## My Role
- Concept development
- Prompt engineering (visuals, voice, style)
- AI content generation
- Editing and storytelling optimization
- Social format adaptation

## Workflow

1. Define hook and message (first 3 seconds are critical)  
2. Generate visuals using AI tools  
3. Create voice or dialogue using AI voice systems  
4. Edit for pacing, realism, and engagement  
5. Optimize for Reels, TikTok, and Shorts  

## Key Focus
- Authentic UGC feel  
- High retention  
- Fast consumption  
- Platform-native storytelling  

## Challenge
Making AI-generated content feel natural and not artificial.

## Solution
- Careful prompt design  
- Human-like pacing  
- Realistic dialogue and tone  
- Subtle editing choices  

## Result
A high-performing UGC-style video created with AI, aligned with modern social media content strategies.
