# JustSmart – Commercial Video Production

## Goal
Create high-performance commercial videos for a delivery app, optimized for social media and advertising.

## My Role
- Video editing
- Storytelling
- Pacing optimization
- Adaptation to multiple formats (16:9 / 9:16)

## Workflow
1. Review raw footage and identify key moments
2. Structure narrative for clarity and engagement
3. Edit for pacing and retention
4. Adapt formats for different platforms
5. Final polish (color, sound, transitions)

## Key Focus
- Retention in the first seconds
- Clean storytelling
- High visual clarity
- Platform adaptation

## Result
Produced multiple commercial videos used for digital campaigns and social media.
